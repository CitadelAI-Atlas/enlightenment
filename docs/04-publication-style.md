# 04 - Publication Style

*Adopted 2026-06-11. These rules govern all published writing in this project:
documents, the site, commit messages, and the repository description.*

1. **Meditation is the frame.** This is meditation-music research. We describe the
   work, its sources, and its goals in the language of meditation, breathwork, and
   receptive music therapy.
2. **Cited literature is described neutrally and precisely.** We name authors,
   institutions, years, and findings. We do not borrow a study's cultural context as
   our own framing.
3. **No duration claims.** Session length is an experimental variable.
   Specifications use normalized percentages of total duration; prose says "medium
   length" until length trials say more.
4. **Sober tone.** No hype, no mysticism presented as mechanism, no claims ahead of
   evidence. Graded labels (evidence-backed / speculative / aesthetic) appear
   wherever claims appear.
5. **Typography.** No em dashes, anywhere. Use commas, colons, parentheses, or
   separate sentences instead. Plain hyphens for numeric ranges (4-8 Hz).
